About AgroFlow

AgroFlow is a digital platform designed to help Krishi Officers manage farmer data, crop planning, harvesting, and sales submissions. With government supervision and field officer input, AgroFlow ensures transparency, efficiency, and fair pricing for farmers across Bangladesh.

Key Features

  • ✅ Officer-managed farmer registration and crop planning
  • ✅ Seasonal harvest tracking and digital field records
  • ✅ Fair pricing system with government monitoring
  • ✅ No middleman involvement—transparent crop sales
  • ✅ Simple and intuitive web interface for all users
